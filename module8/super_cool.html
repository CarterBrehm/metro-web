Welcome to Supah Cool Industries. The goal of this web site is to provide you with up-to-date information about all the Supah Cool things we do. We like to make logos, especially in blue and orange. We like to start the day with coffee and end it with a nice red wine. We like to make photos, especially of memorable images. We travel when we can to talk to other Supah Cool Industry groups around the world. We prefer meeting in places such as Hawaii, Greece, and off the coast of Spain. We probably don't sell a lot of stuff, but we are supah cool, which is really all that matters to us, anyway.
